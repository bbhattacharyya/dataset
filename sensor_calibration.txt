SENSOR CALIBRATION (synthetic benchmark)

Sensor: Tri-ax MEMS accelerometer
Sensitivity: 100 mV/g  (i.e., 0.100 V per g)
Offset (zero-g bias, nominal): 1.650 V  (mid-supply)

Conversion:
  a_g  = (V - 1.650) / 0.100
  a_mps2 = a_g * 9.80665

Notes:
- Polarity follows the sensor printed axes.
- Bandwidth (analog anti-alias): ~2 kHz (2-pole low-pass)
- Use timestamps in shake_table_accel.csv to infer the actual sampling rate.
